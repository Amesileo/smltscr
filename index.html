Welcome!

We are the resident ringers at the Civic and Town church of St Mary-le-Tower in central Ipswich.

There has been a ringing tradition here for over 300 years and certainly from 1896-1939 the St Mary-le-Tower society was one of the leading twelve-bell Companies in the land. Over 700 peals have been rung here, many of them for notable occasions.

We are very grateful to Colin Salter, who is currently researching the history of the SMLT Society. You can read his document here!
